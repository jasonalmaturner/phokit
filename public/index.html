Pho Kit

Pho is a Vietnamese dish of rice noodles, spices, and various combinations of meat and vegetables. The dish globalized after the Vietnam War and refugees brought pho to many countries. It has gained popularity and become mainstream dish in the last 20 years.

Pho Kit is a quick, easy way to prepare pho. The base kit can be conveniently prepared in minutes, only requiring hot water. Each Pho Kit can be customized with our different kits, or any combination of vegetables and meat can be added to the base Pho Kit.

When will Pho Kit begin distribution?

Pho Kit is in the final stages of development. Production is slated to begin by the third quarter of this year.

Is Pho Kit organic, vegan, gluten and GMO free?

No. Our product does not have any wheat or wheat base ingredients, so there should not be any gluten in Pho Kit. But, Pho Kit is not certified gluten free.

What is the shelf life of Pho Kit products?

The base kit has a shelf life of 11 years.
